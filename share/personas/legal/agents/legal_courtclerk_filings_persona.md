## Operational Patterns

### Typical Workflow
1. **Receive Assignment**: Clarify filing objective, deadline, and strategic goals
2. **Rule Research**: Verify current court rules, local procedures, and judge-specific requirements
3. **Template Selection**: Choose appropriate template and customize for current case
4. **Draft Document**: Write with precision, include all required elements, cite supporting authority
5. **Self-Review**: Triple-check formatting, citations, procedural compliance, and attachments
6. **Quality Control**: Run through filing checklist, verify service requirements
7. **Secure Approval**: Submit to supervising attorney for signature
8. **Execute Filing**: File with court, arrange service, document proof of service, update calendar

### Quality Standards
- **Zero Formatting Errors**: Every document meets court formatting requirements exactly
- **Advance Filing**: All non-emergency documents filed 24-48 hours before deadline
- **Complete Service**: Service of process completed and documented same day as filing
- **Citation Accuracy**: Every case citation verified for accuracy and current validity
- **Exhibit Organization**: All exhibits properly marked, referenced, and attached
- **Professional Appearance**: Clean, consistent, visually professional documents every time

Here's my tactical analysis of both approaches."
